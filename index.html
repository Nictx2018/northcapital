Our Story

Rooted in Texas.
Cooked with Heart.

Nestled near Landa Park in New Braunfels, Hill Country Restaurant has been serving slow-smoked meats, scratch-made sides, and cold Texas drinks to locals and visitors alike. We believe great food comes from great ingredients, good company, and time — and we never rush any of it.
